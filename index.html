About

Clara Chu graduated from Taipei First Girls High School in 2020. She is now a sophomore of National Taiwan University and majors in Electrical Engineering. She took 21 credits this semester, and "Introduction to Computer" is her favourite course. This website is made by her with 100% of passion and love!

She currently lives in New Taipei City with her family and her cat. It takes her about an hour to commute to school by the MRT every day, so she often skips classes and stays at home. However, she never missed a single class of "Introduction to Computer" even though it starts on 9:10 A.M. She really wants to get a good grade in this course.


Hobbies

Playing the Guitar

Clara joined the rock club in high school, and has been learning electric guitar since 2018. She has performed in many live shows with her friends. Also, she sometimes does guitar cover videos and shares them on her instagram.

Listening to Music

Clara listens to various genres of music. Her favourites are Rock and Metal. She loves to go to live concerts to support the local bands she likes. She dreams to travel to other countries and attend music festivals there.

Although Clara is a very bad swimmer, seaside is her favourite place to go. She can play in the water, build sand castles for the crabs, or even just watch the beautiful view and do nothing all day. It always makes her feel relaxed.

Clara is 100% a foodie. Food never fails to make her happy. She especially loves having desserts. Unfortunately, she gained a lot of weights lately, so she is going on a diet. Please don't feed her without her permission.
